Finance Lease

A finance lease allows the lessee to hire an asset for a fixed monthly fee, but it also transfers substantially all of the risks and rewards of ownership of the asset to the lessee.

Hire Purchase

Hire Purchase is the most popular way to finance purchasing an asset. Hire Purchase or as it is often referred to "HP" is a fixed method of financing an asset.

Sale & HP Back

Sale and HP Back is where a company owns an asset which has a value. A problem arises when a company needs to raise capital to refinance but cannot afford to sell the equipment because the piece of equipment helps to earn the company an income.

What is Asset Finance?

Leading the way in asset finance Charter Hall is an Asset Finance Company that provides funding solutions for UK Companies. We offer tailored secure finance allowing your business to purchase machinery and equipment. Purchasing the everyday equipment that a company needs in-order to transact business can be extremely costly. In-fact it can drain a business of its cash reserves which can leave a company vulnerable for a variety of reasons. An ideal solution can be Asset Finance.

By spreading the cost of the equipment it means that it can be earning your business valuable income whilst paying for itself. It really can be the smart solution. Alternatively we can also help release the value of your existing fixed assets by re-financing your current equipment, allowing your company the much needed cash to expand or spend the money in other much needed areas.

Charter Hall can arrange Asset Finance to help your business to purchase what it needs. These could include items such as; Office Equipment, Plant and Machinery, Vans and HGV's, even Farm Equipment and Livestock. After business loans and overdrafts, Asset Finance is the most popular way for a company to help finance its future.
